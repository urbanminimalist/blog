    Top 5 Best Quality Kitchen Chef’s Knives of 2017

    2 minute read

    When it comes time to reach for a sharp, clean knife, what’s in your kitchen? Grab the best quality chef’s knife for your culinary journey. Whether you need to break down a chicken, trim a steak of just chop vegetables and herbs, a high quality, sharp, chef’s knife is the best tool in the kitchen. Those of us who like to cook, reach for a clean, sharp knife when its time to get to work. I’ve compiled a list of the top 5 best kitchen chef’s knives.

    1. Wusthof Classic 6-inch Kiritsuke Prep Knife

    At a short, nimble 6 inch blade, the Wusthof works well with limited counter top space. The Wusthof brand has long been known for crafting high quality knives. A versatile, curved edge to facilitate rocking, makes short work of any culinary task.

    2. Victorinox 8 Inch Rosewood Chef’s Knife

    With an affordable price tag and excellent, clean look, the Victorinox is a great choice for chef’s of any skill level. The classic tri-rivet handle design provides optimum weight, balance, and cutting performance, making it feel less like a knife and more like an extension of the hand. Victorinox has been making knives in Switzerland since 1884.

    3. Messermeister San Moritz Elite Chef’s Knife, 8-Inch

    A chef’s knife is the most versatile knife in the kitchen. The curved blade makes quick work of any culinary duty. While Messermeister is the most expensive knife on the list, it’s no surprise, given their the experience as cutlery artisans. The San Moritz is a fine chef’s knife. With decades of expertise making knives, Messermeister brings the highest quality to the chopping block. This knife is best hand washed. Messermeister is from Solingen, Germany.

    4. Enso HD Hammered Damascus 8-inch Chef’s Knife

    Enso HD Hammered Damascus Chef’s Knife is gorgeous. Enso has been making knives since 1932. The Enso’s hammered blade is the most distinct and beautiful of the list. Its use of Damascus steel provides a sharp, resilient edge. The Enso features a double bevel edge for both right and left-handed use. And, it comes with a lifetime warranty.

    5. Mercer Culinary Genesis 8-Inch Forged Chef’s Knife

    The Mercer Genesis is the most affordable of all the chef’s knives on this list. The taper-ground edge allows for added stability, easy honing and long lasting sharpness. The manufacturer recommends that it should be washed by hand for best results.

    Conclusion

    Though it’s nice to have a wide selection of different knives, I only have space for two large knives. Space constraints inherent to apartment life mean trade offs. A chef’s knife is a must have tool for chefs of any skill level. The top 5 best knives isn’t subjective. Knife quality is easily measured using sharpness, hardness and long term quality as guides. Each knife on my top 5 best chef’s knives is a high quality option. Any knife on this list of top 5 best chef’s knives will make quick work of any chopping or cutting task. Whichever knife you purchase, be sure to keep it sharp. Dull knives are the most dangerous knives. Dull knives cause accidents.
