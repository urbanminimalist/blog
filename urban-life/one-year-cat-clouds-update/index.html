    One Year Update on RefinedFeline Cat Clouds

    less than 1 minute read

    Besides a great veterinarian, the best investment for our cats are the Cat Clouds by RefinedFeline. I installed my first Cat Cloud at the aparment in the city a year ago. Read all about the best, minimalist, cat trees of 2017.

    Since the original two clouds, I’ve added two more shelves to our aparment. Our tortise shell lives on the clouds! She takes meals, naps, and watches the world from there. Of course she still sleeps on our pillow at night, but enjoys the clouds while I work. I only wish RefinedFeline made more in the steel line!

    For instance, I’d love to see a steel, wall mounted, vertical stair set to compliment the Cat Clouds and cat silhouettes in steel.
